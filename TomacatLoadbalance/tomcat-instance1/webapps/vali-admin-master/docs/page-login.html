What is VoIP?

VoIP stands for Voice over Internet Protocol and refers to the transmission of voice traffic over Internet-based networks, unlike the traditional hosted PBX.

Am I limited to call only VoIP Phones?

No. Using our robust VoIP services, you can call any phone number in the world, whether it’s a smartphone, desk phone or VoIP call.

How is the Voice Clarity?

Irrespective of the speed of your broadband connection and the device you use, we offer you a high-quality voice clarity on your audio calls.

Do I need a computer to call?

No need for a computer, you can easily install our app on your smartphone or tablet and make calls or send texts at an affordable price.

Why I choose VoIP over Desk Phone?

Using our services, you can save up to 70-80% of your monthly desk phone bill. Moreover, you can install the app on your smartphone and tablet too.

Can I’ve number portability?

Absolutely. You can use the same number that you had on your desk phone. In any view, VoIP service is simple, efficient and cost-effective.
